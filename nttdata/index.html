Striking a balance between responsible, secure AI and innovation

Organizations are under pressure to extract real value from AI. As innovation accelerates, tensions rise between governance and agility — especially in cybersecurity. This webinar explores how to balance responsible and secure AI use with innovation, manage emerging threats like prompt injection and data poisoning, and leverage AI defensively. What you'll learn:

1

Emerging cybersecurity challenges

The risks arriving with the growing adoption of AI — including emerging threats like prompt injection and data poisoning.

2

AI-empowered cyber defenses

How AI is empowering cyber defenses in an AI-enabled threat landscape — and how to leverage AI defensively.

3

The future of cybersecurity

What the future holds for cybersecurity in an AI-driven world, as agentic AI reshapes SecOps.
